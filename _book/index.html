Preface

The NSC-R Workshops is a series of weekly one-hour online instructional sessions to support participants in developing their data science skills in R, and to promote open science principles. The NSC-R workshop meetings are organized by a team affliated with the Netherlands Institute for the Study of Crime and Law Enforcement (NSCR), but they are open to everyone, regardless of affiliation or skill level. All the material of the blog you can find here

The NSC-R Workshop Team

The NSC-R Workshop Team coordinates the NSC-R Workshops. They discuss potential topics, invite presenters, plan the meetings and send invitations. Currently the team includes Wim Bernasco, Franziska Yasrebi-de Kom, Sam Langton, Asier Moneva and Alex Trinidad.

Contact Us and Get involved

If you want your e-mail address on our mailing list, please send an e-mail to Wim Bernasco.

If you have suggestions for workshop topics, or are interested in presenting one, please send a message to Wim Bernasco, Franziska Yasrebi-de Kom, Sam Langton, Asier Moneva or Alex Trinidad.

Harrie Jonkman developed the NSC-R distill website. He also developed this open-source html-book.
